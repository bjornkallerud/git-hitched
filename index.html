Travel Information

Getting There

Nearest Airport: Santa Barbara Airport (SBA) — about 15 minutes to the venue

Los Angeles (LAX): Approximately 1.5–2 hours north on scenic Highway 101. Car rentals are widely available, or take the Santa Barbara Airbus — a direct shuttle service from LAX to downtown Santa Barbara.

SBA is by far the easier arrival, but LAX has far more flight options — both work well

Accommodations

Most of our guests are from Santa Barbara, but for those traveling in, we recommend staying in Santa Barbara for the best experience. Here are options ranging from budget-friendly to luxurious:

  • ⛺ $ — El Capitan & Refugio State Beach (camping)
  • 🌿 $–$$ — El Cap Canyon (glamping cabins & safari tents)
  • 🛏️ $$ — Motel 6, Marina Beach Motel
  • 🏨 $$$ — Hotel Californian, Kimpton Canary, Harbor View Inn
  • ✨ $$$$ — Bacara Resort & Spa (Goleta), Rosewood Miramar

Goleta is closer to the venue but Santa Barbara has more to offer for the weekend

Things to Do

Santa Barbara gets a bad wrap, but it's actually a pretty nice place to be. Some local favorites:

  • Eat a hard boiled egg on Stearns Wharf
  • Eat a hard boiled egg at the top of the courthouse
  • Eat a hard boiled egg at Leadbetter Beach
